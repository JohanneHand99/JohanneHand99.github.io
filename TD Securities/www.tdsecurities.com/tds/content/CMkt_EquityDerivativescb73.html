Equity Derivatives

The Equity Derivatives Group at TD Securities provides a full suite of equity-related investment products, risk management and hedging solutions to serve large and mid-sized corporations and high net worth individuals.

 

To help our clients modify their underlying risk exposures or achieve yield enhancement, we take the time to understand the business needs and challenges they face. With this knowledge, we deliver a wide range of equity derivatives which utilize options, swaps and forwards. The group also creates tailored equity-linked structures designed to achieve a variety of financial objectives relative to a client’s current or prospective equity positions.

 

With our main trading operation in Toronto, and satellite offices in New York, Dublin, London and Singapore, we provide sales and trading services to domestic and international clients. We focus on retail, institutional and corporate distribution.

 

Distinguishing Ourselves From Our Peers

 

TD Securities has been involved in the equity derivatives business since the mid-1990s, earning us a strong position in the Canadian equity derivatives marketplace and a solid global service franchise which draws on the strength of the TD Bank Group.

 

At TD Securities, we offer creativity, insight, depth of professional experience, and the strength of capital required to meet the financing needs of our clients – ranging from the largest, most complex transactions to daily trading and investment facilitation.
