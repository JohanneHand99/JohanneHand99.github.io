Credit Origination

TD Securities is a leading arranger and underwriter of corporate credit products in the Canadian and U.S. credit capital markets. Our fully integrated deal teams combine professionals from industry specialist groups, derivatives, debt and equity capital markets to effectively structure and close transactions on behalf of our clients.

 

Our capabilities include:

  • Bridge and acquisition financing
  • Operating and revolving lines of credit
  • Term loans
  • U.S. and Canadian institutional term loans
  • Commercial paper backup facilities
  • Trade credit products
  • Refinancing, recapitalization and restructuring advisory services
