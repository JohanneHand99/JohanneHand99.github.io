Foreign Exchange

A Leader at Home and Abroad

 

We’re proud of the reputation we’ve earned both at home and farther afield. Domestically, TD Securities is one of the top providers of Canadian dollar spot, forward and currency options. Globally, we’re a leading provider of Canadian dollar product in Asia, Europe and the U.S., with a growing franchise in other "dollar block" Canadian, Australian and New Zealand dollars, as well as the Mexican peso.

 

Making Lasting Connections

 

We value our day-to-day relationships with clients and work hard to build lasting connections so we can satisfy your everyday trading and investment requirements. Our Foreign Exchange sales professionals ensure that we understand your goals and objectives so we can tailor hedging strategies to mirror your specific requirements and risk tolerances.

 

Delivering on Our Promise - Executing with Excellence

 

TD Securities’ clients know they can count on us to deal aggressively and strategically on their behalf, keeping closely connected with you and working hard to exceed your expectations.

 

Value-Added Research

 

To give you a crucial advantage, we offer impressive research capabilities.

  • The daily Market Update from our trading desk provides a commentary on short-term opportunities and market issues.
  • For on-going financial needs and strategic opportunities, our FX Research provides extensive economic analyses of the risks and opportunities in foreign exchange markets. This overview focuses on the medium and longer-term with an emphasis on our Dollar Block expertise.
