Institutional Equities

The Equity Division of TD Securities Inc., is one of Canada’s leading Institutional Equity operations which provides equity sales, trading, research, underwriting and distribution with offices in Toronto, Montreal, Calgary, Vancouver, New York, London, Singapore and Shanghai.

 

Our group resulted from the very successful integration of TD Securities’ equity operation and Newcrest Capital Inc., which was purchased by TD in the fall of 2000. Newcrest Capital was an employee-owned institutional dealer formed in September of 1995 and led by TDSI’s current Chairman, CEO and President, Bob Dorrance.

 

TD Securities Inc. has earned a first rate reputation for quality of execution and integrity with our clients and our substantial capital base serves as a foundation to execute large transactions and provide liquidity for our clients.

 

The Equity Capital Markets Group at TD Securities Inc. is a leader in the origination, structuring and execution of Canadian equity and equity-related new issues. Working in close coordination with Investment Banking and Institutional Equities, the Equity Capital Markets Group has led transactions in a wide range of sectors and securities.

 

To support our other businesses, TD Securities Inc. also brings to the table a top-ranked equity research team. Our analysts provide timely, cutting-edge insights into companies, industries and financial market trends. With an emphasis on communication and client service, our analysts share their expertise to help our customers navigate the North American markets.

 

The Prime Brokerage Group at TD Securities Inc. is one of Canada’s leading service providers in the alternative investment industry. We provide a full suite of services to our clients, which include Hedge Fund Managers, Pension Funds, Family Offices and Corporations.

 

 

TD Securities (USA) LLC is a member of SIPC and FINRA (see FINRA BrokerCheck).

TD Securities Inc. is a member of CIPF and IIROC.
